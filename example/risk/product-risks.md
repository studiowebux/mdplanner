# Product Risk Assessment

## High Impact / High Probability

- Security breach exposing customer data
- Key team member leaving during critical phase
- Beta launch delays affecting investor confidence

## High Impact / Low Probability

- AWS major outage (multi-region failure)
- Legal issues with competitor patents
- Sudden large competitor acquisition

## Low Impact / High Probability

- Minor bugs in beta release
- Feature requests exceeding capacity
- Integration delays with third parties

## Low Impact / Low Probability

- Domain name disputes
- Open source dependency issues
- Minor UI inconsistencies
