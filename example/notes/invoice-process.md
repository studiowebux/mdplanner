# Invoice Process

## Monthly flow
1. Export time entries for the month
2. Create invoice in MDPlanner with line items
3. PDF export → attach to email
4. Send to client by 1st of next month
5. Mark paid when e-transfer received

## Rate card
- Development: $150/hr
- Design review: $100/hr
- Meetings: $75/hr

## Net terms
Net 30 for all clients unless otherwise agreed.

## Late payment
2% per month after 30 days. Send reminder at day 35.
