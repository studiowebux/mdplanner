# Pricing Research

## Competitor pricing
- Linear: $8/user/month (starter)
- Notion: $10/user/month
- Basecamp: $99/month flat
- Height: $8.50/user/month

## MDPlanner positioning
Self-hosted first → free forever for solo.
Cloud hosted → freemium with paid tier.

## Proposed tiers
- **Free** — self-hosted, unlimited everything
- **Cloud Solo** — $9/month — hosted, 1 user, all features
- **Cloud Team** — $19/user/month — hosted, multi-user, SSO

## Key insight
Developers will pay for convenience (no self-hosting) not features.
Price on hosting cost + peace of mind, not feature gates.
