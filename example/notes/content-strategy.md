# Content Strategy

## Goal
Build an audience of developers who manage their own projects solo or in small teams.

## Channels
- Twitter/X — dev tips, build-in-public updates
- Dev.to — technical deep dives
- YouTube — demo videos (screen recordings)

## Content pillars
1. MDPlanner feature launches (monthly)
2. Behind the scenes: building with Deno + htmx
3. Productivity tips for solo devs

## Cadence
- 3x Twitter posts per week
- 1 article per month
- 1 video per quarter

## Metrics
- 500 Twitter followers by Q3
- 100 newsletter subscribers
