## People

- Insufficient QA training
- High staff turnover
- Unclear responsibilities

## Process

- No standardized test procedures
- Missing QA checkpoints in pipeline
- Rework not tracked

## Machine

- Aging test equipment
- Lack of calibration schedule
- Unstable build environment

## Material

- Supplier variability
- Missing material specs
- No incoming inspection

## Method

- Unclear acceptance criteria
- No root cause tracking
- Ad hoc code review process

## Measurement

- Insufficient test coverage
- No defect density metrics
- Delayed defect reporting
