# TaskFlow Lean Canvas

## Problem
- Existing tools are bloated and complex
- Poor UX leads to low adoption
- Expensive for small teams
- No good free tier options

## Solution
- Clean, focused interface
- Quick onboarding (< 5 minutes)
- Generous free tier
- Built-in time tracking

## Unique Value Proposition
Project management that gets out of your way. Simple, fast, affordable.

## Unfair Advantage
- Deep developer experience focus
- Modern architecture (fast, reliable)
- Founder network in tech community

## Customer Segments
- Small tech teams (5-20 people)
- Freelancers and agencies
- Startups
- Remote-first companies

## Existing Alternatives
- Asana (complex, expensive)
- Monday.com (overwhelming UI)
- Trello (too simple, limited)
- Linear (dev-focused only)

## Key Metrics
- Monthly active users
- Tasks created per user
- Team activation rate
- Paid conversion rate

## High-Level Concept
"Notion meets Linear for project management"

## Channels
- Product Hunt launch
- Developer communities
- Content marketing (blog, YouTube)
- Referral program

## Early Adopters
- Developer teams at startups
- Tech-savvy project managers
- Indie hackers and solopreneurs

## Cost Structure
- Cloud infrastructure (~$2k/mo)
- Team salaries
- Marketing spend
- Third-party services

## Revenue Streams
- Freemium SaaS subscriptions
- Team plan: $8/user/month
- Business plan: $15/user/month
- Enterprise: Custom pricing
