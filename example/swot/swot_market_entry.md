## Strengths

- No data residency concerns — runs on user hardware
- Competitive EUR pricing
- Testing

## Weaknesses

- No localized marketing materials
- Support hours limited to North American timezone
- Payment processing only via Stripe (no SEPA direct)

## Opportunities

- EU digital sovereignty initiatives favoring local-first tools
- European developer community underserved by US-centric tools
- Partnership potential with EU open-source foundations

## Threats

- Strong local competitors in Germany and France
- VAT compliance complexity
- Currency fluctuation risk on EUR pricing
